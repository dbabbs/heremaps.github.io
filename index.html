Our open source mission

At HERE, we are dedicated to using location to radically improve the way we get around, the way we live and the way we do business.

To fully realize the potential of location, we need collaboration, partnerships and an open ecosystem. We contribute software to the community to enable input and engagement resulting in the development of better solutions.

We encourage you to explore our projects and collaborate with us in our mission of making location technology accessible to all.
